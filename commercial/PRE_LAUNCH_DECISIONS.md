# Decisions required from the product owner

Complete these before creating live Lemon Squeezy products.

1. **Seller status in Spain** — individual autónomo, company, or another valid structure.
2. **Support email** — a dedicated address that is checked regularly.
3. **Product domain** — a stable domain for plans, policies, support, and the Pro application.
4. **Paid fulfilment model** — account/webhook or licence key.
5. **Minimum Pro feature set** — persistent progress plus at least two additional paid-only capabilities.
6. **Final monthly and annual prices**.
7. **Refund policy** — confirm the draft after professional review.
8. **Target customer age** — adult-only or a defined minor/parental-consent process.
9. **Data processors** — hosting, database, authentication, email, analytics, support, and backups.
10. **Launch cohort size** — recommended first cohort: 10 paying users.
