# OpsReady Pro delivery roadmap

OpsReady Pro should not open checkout until paid access creates a materially better learning product than the public Community edition.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (current)

- stabilise the Community application;
- create a private Pro repository and deployment;
- add learner authentication;
- store progress, assessment history, bookmarks, and subscription entitlement in a database;
- define account deletion, data retention, and support procedures.

## Phase 2 — Founding beta

- expand the reviewed assessment bank;
- add advanced incident and troubleshooting packs;
- add structured multi-session learning paths;
- connect Lemon Squeezy in Test mode;
- verify purchase, access grant, cancellation, expiry, failed-payment recovery, and refund flows;
- onboard an initial cohort of 10 paying learners.

## Phase 3 — Instructor and cohort tools

- instructor dashboards;
- assignments and cohort progress;
- downloadable learner reports;
- organisation licences;
- custom content packs and institution-facing support.

## Minimum live-sales gate

Do not enable live checkout until all of the following work end to end:

1. account creation and sign-in;
2. persistent learner progress;
3. reliable subscription entitlement;
4. cancellation and expiry handling;
5. published terms, privacy, and refund policies;
6. monitored support contact;
7. at least three meaningful Pro-only capabilities.
