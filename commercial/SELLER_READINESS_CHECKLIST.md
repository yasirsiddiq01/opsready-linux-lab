# Seller readiness checklist for Spain-based launch

This is an operational checklist, not legal or tax advice.

## Identity and payout

- [ ] Legal name matches identification and Lloyds account holder name
- [ ] Current Spanish residential address available for verification
- [ ] Valid government-issued identification available
- [ ] Spanish tax identifier and classification confirmed
- [ ] Lloyds sort code and account number available
- [ ] Recent Lloyds statement available if ownership proof is requested
- [ ] Payout and conversion fees reviewed
- [ ] Non-US tax form requested by the platform completed accurately

## Spanish business position

- [ ] Right to conduct self-employed activity under the current immigration/residence status confirmed
- [ ] Autónomo or other appropriate business registration discussed with a Spanish gestor
- [ ] Income-tax and Social Security treatment confirmed
- [ ] Invoicing and bookkeeping process agreed
- [ ] Foreign-account reporting obligations checked where relevant

## Product operations

- [ ] Dedicated support email created
- [ ] Product domain or stable landing page created
- [ ] Private Pro repository created
- [ ] Private Pro deployment created
- [ ] User access and subscription status stored outside browser session
- [ ] Backup and restore process documented
- [ ] Support and incident-response process documented

## Customer information

- [ ] Seller identity published where legally required
- [ ] Total and recurring price clear before purchase
- [ ] Billing interval and cancellation conditions clear
- [ ] Product functionality and limitations clear
- [ ] Compatibility requirements clear
- [ ] Terms published
- [ ] Privacy policy published
- [ ] Refund and cancellation policy published
- [ ] Support contact published

## Launch test

- [ ] Test-mode purchase completed
- [ ] Purchase grants access
- [ ] Duplicate webhook does not create duplicate access
- [ ] Failed signature is rejected
- [ ] Cancellation behaviour verified
- [ ] Expiry behaviour verified
- [ ] Refund behaviour verified
- [ ] Customer portal verified
- [ ] One real low-value live purchase verified before public promotion
