# Refund and Cancellation Policy — Draft

**This is a customer-friendly starting draft and must be checked against the final checkout configuration and applicable law.**

Effective date: `[EFFECTIVE_DATE]`

## Subscription cancellation

Customers may cancel future renewal through the billing portal. Cancellation prevents the next renewal. Access normally continues until the end of the already-paid billing period unless otherwise stated at checkout or required by law.

## Proposed initial-purchase refund window

A customer may request a refund of the first subscription payment within 14 days of purchase by contacting `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]`. This voluntary policy is intended to simplify early customer support and does not remove stronger rights provided by applicable law.

## Technical failure and duplicate payment

A refund or suitable remedy will be provided where:

- the customer was charged more than once for the same purchase;
- paid access was not delivered and could not be restored within a reasonable time;
- the product materially failed to match its published description;
- a refund is required by applicable consumer law.

## Renewal payments

Renewal payments are generally non-refundable once a new billing period has started, except for duplicate charges, material access failures, platform-approved exceptions, or rights required by law. Customers should cancel before the renewal date to avoid the next charge.

## How to request a refund

Email `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]` with the purchase email, order reference, reason for the request, and a description of any access problem. Do not send payment-card details.

## Processing

Approved refunds are processed through the original payment method by the payment platform. Processing time depends on the payment provider and customer's bank.

## Chargebacks

Customers should contact support before opening a payment dispute so access or billing problems can be investigated promptly. This does not restrict any lawful right to dispute an unauthorised transaction.
